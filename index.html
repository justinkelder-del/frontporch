Service flow (quick overview) ⌄
Our gatherings follow a simple, welcoming rhythm: a brief host moment that walks through the flow of the service, a lighthearted “Otis & Friends” moment, worship through music, a message from our teaching team, announcements and offering, then we close by holding hands and singing a benediction as a reminder we go out connected and sent into the week ahead.

Plan a Visit Ask a Question
Who is Front Porch Church for? ⌄
Front Porch Church is for everyone. No matter your age, background, ability, belief, or life experience. You are welcome here. We believe the church is a place of belonging before anything else.
Is there an age range? ⌄
All ages are welcome at Front Porch Church. Children, teens, adults, and older adults are an important part of our community, and we value learning and worshiping together across generations.
Is Front Porch accessible for people with disabilities? ⌄
Yes. Front Porch Church is an all-abilities community. People with disabilities are not just welcomed, they participate, serve, and lead alongside others in the life of the church. If there is something specific you’d like us to know or a way we can better support you or your family, please contact us. We’re always learning and happy to have that conversation.
What if I need to move, make noise, or take breaks? ⌄
That’s okay here. Front Porch values interaction over perfection. Movement, sounds, questions, and breaks are part of how people engage, and you are welcome to participate in the ways that work best for you.
What kind of church is Front Porch? ⌄
Front Porch Church is a Christian community rooted in the life and teachings of Jesus. We are not tied to a specific denomination and seek to be a welcoming place for people from a variety of church backgrounds or none at all.
Do I need to believe a certain way to attend? ⌄
No. You do not need to have everything figured out to belong here. Front Porch is a place for curiosity, exploration, and growth. Wherever you are on your faith journey, you are welcome.
What should I wear? ⌄
Come as you are. There is no dress code. We want you to feel comfortable and at home.
How can I learn more? ⌄
Contact us through the website, talk with a greeter on Sunday, or reach out to a member of our team. We’re always happy to connect.

Our Mission

Front Porch Ministries exists as a Christian community for people of all-abilities who worship, live, work and serve together.

Known • Loved • Valued • Invited into deeper life with God and one another

Core Values (expand to read)

Read our core values ⌄
  • We believe every person is created with inherent worth and dignity.
  • We believe everyone belongs, before they believe or behave.
  • We believe all of us carry strengths and limitations, and all of us need grace.
  • We believe life is meant to be shared, not lived alone.
  • We believe every person has something meaningful to offer the community.
  • We believe our community and world are richer when everyone is welcome to share their gifts and contributions/talents.
  • We believe we are deeply loved by Jesus Christ and reflect the image of God in unique and beautiful ways.
Front Porch Church seeks to follow Jesus Christ by creating a space where people are known, loved, valued and invited into deeper life with God and one another.
