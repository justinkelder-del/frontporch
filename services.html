Our Services

Simple, welcoming gatherings.

Front Porch Church is a community for people of all abilities. Our Sunday service is designed to be clear, participatory, and comfortable for those who may need movement, breaks, or extra support.

Sunday Service

Time: Sunday • 6:00pm – 7:00pm
Location: Yellow Box (Community Christian Church)
1635 Emerson Ln, Naperville IL 60540

Get Directions What to Expect

All Ages & Abilities Welcome

People with disabilities are not just welcomed—they participate, serve, and lead alongside others. If you or your child need to move, make noise, ask questions, or take breaks, that’s okay here. We value interaction over perfection.
