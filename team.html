Our Team

Many gifts. One community.

Front Porch is built by an all-abilities community of people who serve side-by-side to create a clear, welcoming worship experience each week.

How we serve

Our teams are designed so everyone can participate meaningfully. Whether you love tech, hospitality, music, teaching, or simply helping people feel at home—there’s a place for you here.

A/V Team

Sound • Visuals • Tech

The AV Team supports a clear and engaging worship experience by managing sound, visuals, and technical elements during our gatherings. Working behind the scenes, they help create an environment where people can fully participate in worship, teaching, and the community atmosphere of our service.

Teaching Team

Scripture • Learning

The Teaching Team includes people from across our church community, including some with disabilities, who serve by teaching and learning alongside others. Together, they help create welcoming, faith-filled spaces where Scripture is explored and everyone is encouraged to grow.

Worship Team

Music • Prayer

The Worship Team is an all-abilities team made up of people from our church community who help lead us in singing, prayer, and reflection. Together, they create space for joy, honesty, and shared worship as we gather before God.

Welcome Team

Hospitality • Care

The Welcome Team are often the first friendly faces people see at Front Porch Church. With warmth and attentiveness, they help everyone feel noticed, welcomed, and at home, whether it’s your first visit or your hundredth.
