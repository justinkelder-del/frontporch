About Front Porch Church

Everyone belongs here.

Front Porch is a Christian community for people of all abilities who worship, live, work, and serve together. We want to be a place where you’re known, loved, valued, and invited into deeper life with God and one another.

Who We Are

We believe every person is created with inherent worth and dignity. We believe everyone belongs, before they believe or behave. Front Porch is built around a simple idea: the church should feel like a porch — open, welcoming, and relational — where people can show up as they are and find community.

We’re an all-abilities community. People with disabilities are not just welcomed — they participate, serve, and lead alongside others. If you need to move, make noise, ask questions, or take breaks, that’s okay here. We value interaction over perfection.

Our Core Values

We believe every person is created with inherent worth and dignity.
We believe everyone belongs, before they believe or behave.
We believe all of us carry strengths and limitations, and all of us need grace.
We believe life is meant to be shared, not lived alone.
We believe every person has something meaningful to offer the community.
We believe that our community and world are richer when everyone is welcome to share their gifts and contributions/talents.
We believe we are deeply loved by Jesus Christ and reflect the image of God in unique and beautiful ways.

Front Porch Church seeks to follow Jesus Christ by creating a space where people are known, loved, valued and invited into deeper life with God and one another.
